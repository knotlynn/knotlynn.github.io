Ice cream recipe

  1. mix together 2 tablespoons of vanilla, 2 teaspoons of cinnamon, and 1 8oz can of condensed milk
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk 2 cups of heavy cream until it reaches stiff peakes
  3. Gently fold the condensed milk misture into the whipped cream
  4. Pour the mixture into a freezer safe container
  5. Freeze the ice cream for 6 hours or until solid all the way through
  6. Enjoy!
